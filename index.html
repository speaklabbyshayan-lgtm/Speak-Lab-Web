Everything you need to know about the program

It is an intensive 8-week English communication course held at Punjab Tianjin University of Technology, Lahore. Classes run Monday, Wednesday, and Friday from 5:00 PM to 6:45 PM — 24 total sessions designed to transform your spoken English and build real confidence.

This program is for students, fresh graduates, and young professionals in Pakistan who want to speak English confidently in interviews, workplaces, and everyday conversations.

You will master Spoken English, Professional Communication, Public Speaking, Interview Preparation, Critical Thinking, and Confidence Building — all through hands-on practice.
